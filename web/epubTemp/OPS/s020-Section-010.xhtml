17 July - 28 July 1941


Settling into a common pattern raiding Bengasi (Bengazi) Harbour. This was regarded as essential to the Africa Campaign. It changed control from Axis to Allied powers five times during the war.

The British Army finally took the Port of Benghasi in 1942, during the Battle of El Agheila.

Mission 7. 20 June 1941. Raiding docks and shipping at Bengasi Harbour.

Mission 8. 24 June 1941. Raid on Bengasi

Mission 9. 28 June 1941. Raid on Bengasi.
