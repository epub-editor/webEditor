2 February - 3 February 1942

Posted to the UK.


On the 3 February 1942 Ken was posted to the U.K.. His 45th Mission and last in Africa was a rraid on transport and infrastructure. He then flew from Landing Ground 15 (LG15) to Heliopolis and on to Kabrit.

Mission 45. 2 February 1942. Ajadabia Antelat. Troops, Tanks, etc.
