15 July - 31 July 1942

Posted to Bassingbourn 11 O.T.U.


There is a gap from February to 15th July with no flying.

Ken is now at Bassingbourn 11 Operational Training Unit (OTU) where it appears training on new navigation equipment is underway.

July 1942 Flying Hours: 14hrs 45mins.
