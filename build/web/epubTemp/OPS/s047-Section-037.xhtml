15 July - 25 July 1943

Night flying on what was known as a "Bullseye" operation, which were designed to simulate as close as possible an operational night flight.

"Gardening Flights" were real mine-laying operations around the Friesian Islands off the Dutch/German coast. All mine laying flights were given this label.

July 1943 Flying Hours: Night: 14hrs 50mins. Day: 10hrs 45mins.
